Inverted Pendulum Details

Designed and implemented a proportional-integral-derivative integral (PID) controller to stabilize an inverted pendulum mounted on a moving car, ensuring the pendulum recovered from disturbances and maintained balance by controlling the motor to drive and reverse the car. Conducted rigorous testing, addressing challenges like jitter, drift, and hardware limitations to achieve a functional and responsive stabilization system.
